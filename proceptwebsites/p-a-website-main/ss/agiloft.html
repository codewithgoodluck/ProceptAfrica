CLM FOR SALES

Shorten the sales cycle and boost revenue. The Full Salesforce integration enables instant generation of sell-side documents like proposals, MSAs, and other agreements.

CLM FOR PROCUREMENT

Automate your entire contract lifecycle. Ensure every contract complies with corporate standards with a digital repository, clause library, and configurable business rules engine.

CLM FOR LEGAL

Get efficient and reduce risk in your contract processes. AI-enabled risk scoring surfaces the risk in your contracts so you can focus on and minimize high-risk contracts.

CLM Features

Take full advantage of Agiloft’s comprehensive features and benefits through the CLM suite

Contract Repository

Get immediate access and visibility to all your organization's contracts. Configurable dashboards, charts, reports, saved views provide repository information in flexible formats.

Template and Clause Libraries

Create contracts instantly using standardized approved language with contract templates. Also manage language across contracts by clause type, usage, priority, status, team, owner, contract templates or any other metadata.

Security

Keep your data safe from bad actors with an application and hosting infrastructure validated by 3rd party security firm audits. Protect your data from disaster with a doubly redundant infrastructure. In addition to redundant servers at the primary data center, it is replicated to slave servers over 500 miles away.

Approval Workflows and Execution

Automate even the most complex approval routing process including a combination of sequential, parallel, and conditional approvals. Manage workflows with outside parties through vendor/client portals using integrated email and SMS messaging.

Compliance

Comply with regulatory requirements like Sarbanes-Oxley, GDPR and HIPAA while managing risk, with precise controls, and a complete audit trail for every contract. Take advantage of robust compliance analytics out-of-the-box, or easily build reports to capture custom metrics.

Artificial Intelligence

Recognize and import meta data such as contract amount and renewal date from scanned documents using pre-built AI functions. Boost productivity on the road through spoken language integration with Alexa. Shorten cycles by automating tasks with custom AI models built on our open architecture integration with AWS SageMaker and TensorFlow

Alerts and Performance Tracking

Ensure the timely execution of key processes with automated alerts, escalations and assignments through integrated email and SMS messaging. Measure KPI's and spot bottlenecks with comprehensive dashboards and reports.
