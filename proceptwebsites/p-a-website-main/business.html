Case Study

Business Analysis and Business Relationship Management

Requirements

Our partner introduced us to a client whose need was to distinguish between the role of Business Analysis and Business Relationship Management. We were required to develop a custom one-day workshop to address their needs. The workshop was to be delivered to senior and executive management within and outside IT and in Abuja.

Solution

Procept worked with the sponsor of the assignment using our courseware solution methodology and course design approach to prepare a one-day workshop. The workshop which was delivered to 32 senior and executive management by our CEO, received excellent participants' feedback.

Outcomes

The outcome of the project improved the user's ability to:

  • Recognize the meaning, context, and value that Business Analysts bring to the business
  • Automatically generate test cases and scenarios from diagrams, and use cases
  • Understand the differences between a Business Analyst, Business Relationship Manager, Financial Analyst, Project Manager, and other analysis related roles
  • Recognize business analysts tools and techniques
  • Be aware of the approach for maturing business analysis practice
