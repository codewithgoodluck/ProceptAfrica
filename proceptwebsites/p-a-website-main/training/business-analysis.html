Overview

Procept Africa offers Business Analysis courses, enabling participants to understand and implement a wide range of problem-solving techniques that can be applied to deduce any business problem. Each course can be taken individually or as part of a program towards certification. Instructional time per course ranges from 14–28 hours and each is offered on-site or on-line training, at one of our partnered universities, or through other public classroom and online deliveries.
