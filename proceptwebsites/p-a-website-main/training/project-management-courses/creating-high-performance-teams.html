Abstract

Prepare to meet the challenges of 21st century by creating high performance teams in a global economy. Virtual teams are now a reality in the workplace and an eﬀective virtual team worker will be a valued asset. Today, many project managers must be able to lead virtual teams with conﬁdence.

When high performance requires multiple skills and experiences, nothing is more vital than a project team empowered to succeed. Virtual teams and multi generational workforce are now a reality and project managers in present global economy must appreciate the additional risks and challenges in managing a virtual team and a mix of multi generational team members. Understanding and applying appropriate team dynamics will give you the ﬂexibility and speed you need to create high performing team to withstand tough global competition.

Real teamwork and eﬀective communications are vital for creating high performance teams. High team performance is a by-product of eﬀective team leadership. In this seminar, you will learn the process of eﬀective team building, developing quality-driven project teams and practice the art of leading virtual teams. You will evaluate the eﬀectiveness of your project teams and discover practical tips to reﬁne your team building and team leadership skills to maintain and inspire high team performance throughout the project life cycle in a global economy.

Learning

Using small-group activities, skill practices, case studies, lectures, diagnostic questionnaires, and group discussions, participants will gain practical skills to:

  • Recognize the stages of project team development and enhance the process of team building.
  • Evaluate and inﬂuence team dynamics and team eﬀectiveness.
  • Organize project teams for success by developing eﬀective team communications.
  • Identify drivers and barriers to eﬀective team building and develop appropriate solutions.
  • Recognize and capitalize on cultural diversity to manage international projects.
  • Develop appropriate team leadership styles and strategies for global projects.
  • Develop skills to manage virtual teams in a global project environment.
  • Develop and create high performance teams by optimizing human synergy in a global economy.

Who Should Attend

This seminar is for Project Managers, Portfolio and Program Managers, Project Team Members, and Technical Personnel (at all experience levels) who want to build eﬀective project teams and achieve human synergy. It is especially recommended for those who are facing the challenges of developing high performance teams in a global environment.

There is no prerequisite for this course

An examination will be done at the end of this course.
