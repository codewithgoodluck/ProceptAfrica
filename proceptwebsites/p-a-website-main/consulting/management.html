Overview

Procept's team of consultants provides management consulting services in several areas including organizational design, business strategy consulting, designing employee rewards and recognition programs, and business continuity planning.

Transforming Business Systems & Performance

The BusinessDictionary.com defines a Business System as a methodical procedure or process that is used as a delivery mechanism for providing specific goods or services to customers. The Law Dictionary defines Business System as a combination of people and automated applications organized to meet a particular set of business objectives. We define business systems as a combination of people, policies, processes, procedures, data and information, working together to achieve a set of business objectives.

With over fifteen yearâs industry experience, Procept will work with Clients to transform their business systems and performance.

Business Systems Effectiveness Review (BSER)

The Business Systems Effectiveness Review (BSER) is a subset of our Transforming Business Systems and Performance (TBSP), and it entails assessing business systems for revenue and cost optimization, with the ultimate goal of improving the productivity per man and doing more for less. It assesses revenue drivers to identify revenue leakages, while identifying opportunities to significantly increase the revenue of the business, while adopting guiding principles as fairness and equity, without compromising its corporate responsibility to all its stakeholders including its community at large

Our strategic planning retreat is a subset of our Business Strategy Formulation and Management assignment, and we work with senior and executive management teams to facilitate the creation of business strategy outputs at an annual event hosted by our client.
