Overview

Procept consultants have been helping organizations develop and implement projects, programs, and portfolio management frameworks, methods, and tools for almost 30 years. Deliverables are dependent on industry best practices and as well as the most current developments in project management from PMI, IPMA, APMG, and ISO.

Project Governance Establishment

Project Governance refers to the set of policies, regulations, functions, processes, procedures, and responsibilities that define the establishment, management, and control of projects. It is a subset of corporate governance, but unique to the project environment.

Preparing a Project Management Methodology

Procept consultants help organizations identify the right methodologies for projects, and then adapt them to the individualized business environment, smoothening the transition to the new methods with phased adoption road maps. Procept can also change existing methodologies to improve project efficiency while still preserving necessary project oversight.

PM Competency Assessments

Tap into years of experience of assessing project managers of all experience levels to measure their competence against international standards. Procept provides individualized recommendations for skills development and project management career planning­ — a valued reward for your star performers.

Project Management Coaching and Mentoring

Procept provides several offerings to address the needs of Project Managers who require additional help or support. These offerings are meant to verify and support the project manager's knowledge and application of competence in each project phase.

Project Management Practice Assessment

This entails assessing the project management practice of an organization including its organizational structure, processes, technology, as well as assessing the practice's overall effectiveness to the organization.

Project Planning

Project planning is a part of project management which relates to the use of schedules to plan and subsequently report progress within the project environment.

Project Management Office Establishment

A Project Management Office is an organizational entity operating a centralized and coordinated management of projects within its domain.

Project Audits

These are a systematic process used to identify the reasons why project stalls or fails, as well as the documentation of results and processes used for a successful project which can be replicated in the future.
